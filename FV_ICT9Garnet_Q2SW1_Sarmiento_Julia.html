Chow Chow

Chow Chows are known for their lion-like features. They are quiet, clean and friendly. They are known for the unique color of their tongues. A Chow Chow's tongue is usually blue or dark blue.

Maltese

Maltese is a dog breed that is easy to train. They are known for their tiny, little and cute appearance and size. Commonly, white is the color of the fur of a Maltese.

Poodle

This breed is known as one of the smartest. A Poodle is known for their curly fur too. Their life span is around 10-18 years.
